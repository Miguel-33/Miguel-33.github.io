Tusca R.S. Alexis

Tusca R.S. Alexis is a practicing attorney in Tennessee and a Rule 31-listed family and civil mediator, with specialized training in domestic violence. She earned her undergraduate degree from Adelphi University in Long Island, New York, in 1986, and her law degree from Drake University School of Law in Des Moines, Iowa, in 1989. Ms. Alexis has been practicing law since 1990, focusing on family law, personal injury, and criminal law. She is also a proud member of the Tennessee Bar Association's Alternative Dispute Resolution Section.
