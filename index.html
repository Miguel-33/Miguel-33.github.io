Tusca R. S. Alexis

Tusca R.S. Alexis is a Rule 31-listed family and civil mediator in Tennessee, specializing in conflict resolution and negotiation. With extensive experience in mediation, she offers expertise in domestic violence mediation and family disputes. Ms. Alexis is committed to guiding clients through challenging situations with a focus on peaceful resolution. She holds an undergraduate degree from Adelphi University (1986) and a law degree from Drake University School of Law (1989). Ms. Alexis has been practicing law since 1990, and she is an active member of the Tennessee Bar Association’s Alternative Dispute Resolution Section.
