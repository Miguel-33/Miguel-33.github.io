What is Mediation?

Mediation is a voluntary, confidential process where a neutral third-party mediator helps disputing parties openly discuss their issues and work toward a mutually acceptable agreement. Unlike traditional litigation, mediation is flexible and collaborative, empowering both parties to make decisions that best meet their needs.

Why Choose Mediation?

  • Cost-Effective: Less costly than court proceedings.
  • Confidential: Private sessions allow for open and honest discussions.
  • Time-Saving: Faster resolutions compared to litigation.
  • Preserves Relationships: Ideal for family, business, and personal disputes.
