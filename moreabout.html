Tusca R.S. Alexis

Tusca R.S. Alexis is a practicing attorney since 1990, and a member of the Tennessee Bar. Alexis is a Rule 31 listed Family Mediator, General Civil Mediator and is specially trained in domestic violence. Alexis also provides Continuing mediator education courses for mediators in the Middle Tennessee area, and continuing legal education hours for Attorneys.

Alexis obtained her undergraduate education at Adelphi University in Long Island, New York in 1986, and her law degree from Drake University School of Law in Des Moines, Iowa in 1989. She has practiced as an attorney for over 30 years and has experience in the areas of family law, personal injury law, and criminal law. Alexis is a member of the Tennessee Bar Association Alternative Dispute Resolution Section and the American Bar Association.
