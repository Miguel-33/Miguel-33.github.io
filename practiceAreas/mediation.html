What is Mediation?

Mediation is a voluntary, confidential process where a neutral third-party mediator assists disputing parties in discussing their issues openly and working toward a mutually acceptable agreement. Unlike traditional litigation, mediation allows for flexibility and collaboration, empowering both parties to make decisions that best meet their needs.

Why Choose Mediation?

  • Cost-Effective: Mediation is often less costly than going to court, making it a practical choice for many.
  • Confidential: Unlike court proceedings, mediation sessions are private, allowing parties to speak freely and confidentially.
  • Time-Saving: Mediation typically takes less time than court cases, enabling faster resolutions and less disruption to your life.
  • Preserves Relationships: Ideal for family disputes, business conflicts, and other situations where maintaining relationships is beneficial.
